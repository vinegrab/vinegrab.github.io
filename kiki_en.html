Join KiKi to start sand painting! Use beautiful sands to paint the fun coloring pages of animals, dinosaurs and fairy tale characters. It inspires kids’ sense and recognition for colors and objects, help bring out creativity and hands-on ability of the kids.

Features:

  • Three coloring page themes in lovely animals, dinosaurs and fairy tale characters.
  • Want to recolor? Use the shovel tool to remove the existed sand and re-paint.
  • Choose between Sand Spraying and Sand Shaking modes.
  • Parent center to let you set play time and get analysis for the kid’s favorite color.
  • Easily share to Facebook, Instagram etc. to share with family and friends.
